About me

Hi! I’m Shalu, a UI designer with website design skills.

I have a Master's in computer application and a background in website and assistant designer, which has prepared me to become a UI/Visual designer. As an web designer, I developed new skills to design the web pages according to the user’s needs and wants, analyze their problems, and provide the best solutions. I interacted with colleagues and other teams while I was an Assistant designer. So I understand to collaborate with teams and clients. I learned to design the mobile interface according to the project requirements and client needs. Although I have always been interested in photography and drawing for creative work, I spend my day creating designs for fun in photoshop. I always like to communicate with people, and my curiosity about human behaviour allows me to develop intuitive, clean and aesthetically pleasing human-centred designs.
